School Profile

“Learning Today Leading Tomorrow”

Yashvardhan public school Isagarh road,kadwaya. established in 2014. A Private Co-Ed day School located in the sylvan surrounding of Royal City. Our Institution brings you a set of Luxurious as well as comfortable setting (Environment), an atmosphere conductive to learn for children. We have given all due attention to the ever changing technologies & Methodologies which have a Strong Impact on teaching.

Priority is given to planned spaces for extra ordinary rooms with all necessary facilities, Like Easy Transportation, Easy Monitoring, Proper control and tight security for the protection of children.

Affiliation Status

The school is recognized by Madhya Pradesh Education Department and affiliated to CBSE New Delhi vide Affiliation No. 8575383.




Our Aim and Objetives

“Children must be taught how to think, not what to think.”

Our Primary and most important aims is to make the overall developement of the child. To promote and encourage humanitarian values of love Justice, Respect Honesty, Services and trust throughoutr the School Community. Yashvardhan Public School would encourage its educationalist and learners to speak in English in formal as well as in informal school environment. Technology Based Language Laboratries would be used to improve their proficiency in Reading, Writing, Pronunciation and Fluency. Our efforts are to encourage all children to realize their true potential and seek educational challenges. We stand committed to ‘Service Before Self’ and prepare the young learners to act in accordance with a personal philosophy that reflects concern for all and understand the ethical implications of the various disciplines.

Curriculum

“Making Your Child's World Better”

SGVS Sironj follows the CBSE syllabus. A synergistic mix of Sports, Music, Dramatics, Dance and Computers with academic classes completes the picture of overall growth at SGVS Sironj.

The curriculum has been evolved to achieve a balance between the emphasis accorded to academics and the importance of development of the body and the mind.

Academic Programme

“Education is the manifestation of perfection already existing in man”

Yashvardhan public school Isagarh road,kadwaya follows a pattern of three years of schooling. Starting with one year of Nursery to Class 8th after that then 3 years of Secondary and Senior Secondary School, based on the 10+2 CBSE pattern of instruction.
